---
description: How the salon owner assigns a stylist to a booking
---

This workflow describes the process where a booking with "Any available" stylist preference is assigned to a specific professional by the salon owner or manager.

1. **Booking Notification**: The salon owner receives a notification of a new booking with "Any available" stylist preference.
2. **Review Booking**: Open the XalonHub partner app and navigate to the "Today's Bookings" or "Requested Bookings" section.
3. **Select Booking**: Click on the specific booking to see details.
4. **Identify Available Stylists**: Check which stylists are available for the requested services, date, and time slot.
5. **Assign Stylist**: Click on "Assign Stylist" and select the appropriate professional from the list of available stylists.
6. **Confirm Assignment**: The booking status will update to "Confirmed" once a stylist is assigned, and the customer will be notified via the Xalon app.
7. **Service Start**: When the customer arrives, the assigned stylist begins the service.
